## _This mod brings new structures full of random loot depending on rarity!_
---
### Features
- Currently around 15-20 lootable structures.
- Powerful loot.
- Structures for the End & Nether.
- Rare loot pools.
- Makes exploring more incentive.
- Perfect for modpacks!
